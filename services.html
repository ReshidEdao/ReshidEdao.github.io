OUR SERVICES ARE:

Awash Bank of Negelle Arsi, Kiltu Dema Branch offers a wide range of financial services for both individuals and businesses. Here are some of the key services they provide:

Personal Banking


Savings Accounts: Various savings account options to help individuals save money.
Checking Accounts: Convenient checking accounts for everyday transactions.
Loans: Personal loans, automobile loans, mortgage loans, and consumer loans.
Money Transfers: Domestic and international money transfer services.
Digital Banking: Internet and mobile banking services for easy account management.

Business Banking


Business Accounts: Checking and savings accounts tailored for businesses.
Loans and Financing: Short-term loans, overdraft facilities, and long-term financing for large projects.
Trade Services: Letters of guarantee and other trade-related financial services.
Money Transfers: Efficient domestic and international money transfer services.

Specialized Services


Diaspora Accounts: Accounts designed for Ethiopians and foreigners of Ethiopian origin living abroad.
Interest-Free Banking: Sharia-compliant banking services.
School Tuition Fee Collection: Services for collecting school fees.
Investment Services: Various investment options for individuals and businesses.

Additional Services


ATM and POS Services: 24/7 access to funds through ATMs and point of sale terminals.
Agency Banking: Banking services through authorized agents.
Customer Support: Dedicated customer support for all banking needs.
These services are designed to cater to the diverse needs of their customers, ensuring accessibility and convenience
