HISTORY OF COMPANY:

Awash Bank, one of Ethiopia’s largest private banks, was established on November 10, 1994, by 486 founding shareholders with a paid-up capital of Birr 24.2 million. The bank began its operations on February 13, 1995. Over the years, Awash Bank has grown significantly, with its paid-up capital increasing to over Birr 5.87 billion and the number of shareholders exceeding 4,369 by the end of June 2020. The Kiltu Dema branch is one of the many branches of Awash Bank, which has expanded its network to over 700 branches across Ethiopia. While specific details about the history of the Kiltu Dema branch are not readily available, it is part of the bank’s extensive efforts to provide accessible banking services throughout the country.
